Rules & Regulations

1. General Conduct

  • All players must adhere to the spirit of cricket.
  • The umpire's decision is final and binding. Any dissent will result in disciplinary action.
  • Teams must report to the ground at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Players must wear appropriate cricketing attire.

2. Tournament Format

The tournament will follow a round-robin league format, followed by knockout stages (Semi-Finals and Final).

  • Each match will be 10 overs per side.
  • A win grants 2 points, a loss 0 points. In case of a tie, a Super Over will decide the winner.
  • Top four teams from the league stage will qualify for the Semi-Finals.

3. Playing Rules

  • A maximum of 3 overs can be bowled by a single bowler.
  • Powerplay will be the first 3 overs, with only two fielders allowed outside the 30-yard circle.
  • Free hits will be awarded for all no-balls (overstepping).
  • In case of a rain interruption, the Duckworth-Lewis-Stern (DLS) method will be applied if a minimum of 5 overs have been completed in the second innings.

4. Code of Conduct

  • No abusive language or sledging will be tolerated.
  • Damage to property or equipment will result in immediate disqualification and fines.
  • Consumption of alcohol or illegal substances is strictly prohibited on the premises.
